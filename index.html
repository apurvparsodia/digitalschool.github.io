Your children safety is our first priority

The information about student such as in time and out time from Bus and campus will be recorded to web based system and SMS will be automatically sent to their parents that the student arrived to Bus/Campus safely. SMS will be sent to parents whose children are absent without taking leave. The parents can log into system website and monitor the details of their children. The implementation of School Security System(SSS) via RFID to avoid crime, illegal activates by students and reduce worries among parents.
